Heading

Buttons allow users to take significant action–submit a form, follow a user, create a record etc.

Default heading

Occasionally, if an action is more significant, essential or impactful, one of three pronounced buttons can be used.

I am a default heading

Title

The primary heading in a screen or product. Often used on the homepage of a marketing site. Never use this more than once per screen.

I am a title

Large heading

A main heading of a screen or large section of a screen. Often used to marketing site sections. Can be used multiple times

I am a large heading

Subheading

Subheadings provide extra context for a large heading or title. A wrapper div is required to provide margin for the subheading.

Large heading

I am a subheading accompanying a large heading.

The subheading can go on top of the large heading. Make sure whichever heading is on top is shorter. The gray color modifier is commonly used to subdue the subheading but any color modifier can be used.

Subheading

Large heading

A subheading can also be used to support a title, for example in a marketing site hero section. When supporting titles, subheadings need a larger margin. If you like, you can also centerby heading groups by wrapping them in a <div class="ta-center">.
